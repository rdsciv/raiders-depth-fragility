## Source usage

Sources are attached at the row level:

- Draft picks use the live Raiders tracker, draft order, and team announcement sources.
- Roster players use the official Raiders roster.
- Depth assessments use official roster, depth-chart availability, and NFL IQ concept sources.
- Scheme roles use staff context, NFL IQ concepts, and the local research brief.
- Prospects use NFL IQ-style and public fallback prospect sources.
